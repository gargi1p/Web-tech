Breakfast Recipe: Pancakes

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Cooking time: 10 minutes

Fluffy pancakes perfect for a quick breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the egg and milk together.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and stir until combined.
  4. Heat a pan and pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.

Lunch Recipe: Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Preparation time: 5 minutes | Cooking time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place the cheese between the unbuttered sides.
  3. Cook in a pan over medium heat until both sides are golden brown and cheese is melted.
